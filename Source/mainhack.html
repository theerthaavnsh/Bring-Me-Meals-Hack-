About Us

"Bring me meals" is a hybrid food donation platform founded in 2022 to solve the hunger crisis in the city by MEC students. This platform allows you to donate food where we find the people in need near your location and deliver food packets to them. Join our community by volunteering to give back to the society that nurtured you!
